GAME OF PIG

There are two players. Each takes turn rolling the dice. Points are accumulated during a players turn, and can be offered to THE PIG, ending the turn. Rolling double 1's will awaken THE PIG's wrath and your ALL offerings will be destroyed. Rolling a single 1 will forfeit the player's offerings this turn and the next player will begin their tribute. The first player to provide THE PIG 100 offerings will descend into blissful, unending filth.

THE PIG WILL DECIDE WHO SHALL PROCEED
